SUHASA
Early Childhood Learning Center

Mission

Intentionally building a collaborative learning community while celebrating the strengths of all learners and supporting the growth of early learning skills through developmentally appropriate practices to build a foundation for learning.

Vision

Creating and maintaining a safe, supportive, and nurturing learning environment for all children, families, and staff promoting social/emotional competence as a foundation for developing the whole Child.

Core Values

  • Ongoing Learning
  • Reflective Practice
  • Collaboration
  • Inclusive Environment
  • Family Involvement
