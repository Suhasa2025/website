Day Care

Our Day care program is designed to create a nurturing, safe, and stimulating environment for babies aged 6 weeks to 1 year. Our focus is on:

Nurturing Care

Experienced caregivers who provide warmth, attention, and personalized care to meet each infant’s needs.

Sensory Exploration

Activities designed to engage babies' senses through touch, sight, sound, and movement, helping them discover the world around them.

Foundational Development

Supporting early milestones such as rolling, sitting, crawling, and beginning communication through songs, stories, and play.

Individual Attention

Low caregiver-to-infant ratios to ensure each child gets dedicated interaction and care.

Daily Routines

Structured routines for feeding, napping, and playtime to help infants feel secure and develop good habits.
