Ages 18 months - 3 years

Pre Kinder Garden I & II

Our Pre kinder garden program is designed to provide a safe, nurturing, and stimulating environment for children aged 18 months to 3 years. Our comprehensive approach focuses on:

  • Developing safe and healthy habits
  • Fostering positive self-esteem
  • Providing opportunities for creative expression
  • Supporting the development of social skills
  • Stimulating cognitive problem-solving
  • Strengthening listening, reading, writing, and speaking skills
  • Enhancing fine motor and gross motor skills
  • Assessing individual learning needs
