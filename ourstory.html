About Us

Sri Satyanarayana Educational Society, having over 40 years of experience in the field, is committed to providing quality education for children and creating a positive change in society by launching Suhasa, a premium early childhood learning center.

Why Choose Us

We aim to create a premium hands-on learning environment where children develop not only academically but also emotionally, socially, and physically.

We Value

Individualized attention, inquiry-based learning, nurturing creativity, experiential learning, and holistic development.

Our Facilities

Bright, welcoming classrooms, outdoor play areas, and dedicated music and art spaces ensure a safe and engaging environment.

It's a Combined Effort

We believe in strong collaboration between parents and teachers to foster the best environment for child development.
