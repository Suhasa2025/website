Founders Story

"Sri Satyanarayana Educational Society" having over 40 years of experience in the field, is committed to providing quality education for children and creating a positive change in society by launching SUHASA, a premium early childhood learning center. At SUHASA early childhood learning center, our belief is that every child is unique, and the aim is to nurture and promote the holistic development of every child through "Activity and experiential-based learning."
