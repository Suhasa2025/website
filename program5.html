Ages 3-4 years

Kinder Garden I & II


Our Kinder garden program is designed to prepare children aged 3-4 years for their educational journey ahead. Our comprehensive curriculum focuses on:

Language and Literacy Development

Listening and speaking, vocabulary building, pre-reading skills, and understanding rhyme and rhythm.

Mathematical Development

Number recognition, problem-solving skills, and understanding shapes and sizes.

Self-Regulation

Managing emotions and learning to express feelings appropriately.

Social Skills

Sharing, taking turns, and developing cooperation with peers.

Following Instructions

Understanding and following simple instructions and daily routines.

Self-Help Skills

Practicing independence through age-appropriate self-help activities.

Physical Development

Enhancing gross motor skills and developing fine motor coordination.

Creative Development

Fostering imagination and creativity through exploration and discovery.

Family Engagement

Building strong home-school connections and encouraging parental involvement.
